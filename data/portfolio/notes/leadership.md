# Leadership and Collaboration

## Leadership Themes
- Senior / lead frontend ownership
- Mentorship
- Cross-team collaboration
- Agile delivery leadership
- Code quality advocacy
- Platform and standards development

## Examples
- Led and mentored distributed engineering teams across Agile environments.
- Led teams of 4 and 5 developers on enterprise-scale React and C# projects.
- Established design systems and shared frontend standards.
- Improved developer velocity through reusable platforms, code reviews, and mentoring.
- Contributed internal technical guidance and best practices education.
- Supported sprint planning, backlog refinement, and cross-team delivery in remote environments.
- Mentored developers and contributed to architecture decisions that improved code quality, accessibility, and team velocity.
- Established frontend standards across React, TypeScript, testing, state management, and reusable UI patterns.

## Management and Team Impact
- Improved delivery speed across teams
- Reduced UI defects
- Strengthened accessibility and testing culture
- Increased release confidence through CI/CD and testing standards
